## Questions to clarify before applying

For ambiguous postings, ask:
- "Is the comp + level worth pursuing for this specific posting?"
- "Does this company actually meet your 'interesting' bar?"
- "Is the role description specific enough about day-to-day work, or is it vague in a way that suggests the team doesn't know?"
