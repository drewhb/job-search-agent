## Schema notes

- **Channel:** LinkedIn, Email, Referral, Cold outreach, etc.
- **Status:** Replied, Awaiting reply, Phone screen scheduled, Passed, Moved to formal application
- When a recruiter conversation turns into a real application, **also** add a row to `applications-log.md` and create the `applications/<Company>_<Role>/` folder.

## Useful context to capture

For each recruiter conversation, when you paste the message, also note:
- Whether the recruiter is internal (works at the company) or external (agency)
- Comp range or level if they shared it upfront
- Specific role details they mentioned that aren't in the public JD
